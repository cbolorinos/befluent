How it works Personalized and convenient

With BeFluent you take complete control of your studies and as a result enhance the opportunities available to you both professionally and socially. With the choice of learning either English or Spanish via Skype, you learn a language at your convenience and from the comfort of your own home.

In addition to convenience, BeFluent provides its students with qualified and experienced native speakers as their teachers. You can even choose what country your teacher comes from! Therefore no matter where you are you have access to the best language instruction available.

About us Breaking barriers

We allow our students to personalize their lessons as much as possible while maintaining teaching principles that ensure our students end up fully capable of accomplishing what they set out to do. And by taking a free introductory lesson, we can verify our student's language leveland ensure they receive the proper instruction.

Oftentimes it is hard to access native and bilingual speakers of a language, especially if you are not in a country where the language is spoken. With BeFluent we break down those barriers and bring our students natural experts in the language they want to master.

At BeFluent we are well-versed in the certification exams for English and Spanish and thus are able to produce course plans that follow accordingly.

Benefits Talk Like a Local
